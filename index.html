Chef Jean Baptiste

Executive Chef & Owner

With over two decades of culinary excellence, Chef Baptiste brings his French training and global perspective to create unforgettable dining experiences. Trained under Michelin-starred chefs in Lyon and Paris, he opened Bistro Elegante with a vision to celebrate both tradition and innovation.

Our Story

A legacy of culinary excellence in the heart of Manhattan

Founded in 1995 by Chef Jean Baptiste and his wife Marie, Bistro Elegante began as a dream to create an intimate space where culinary artistry could flourish. What started as a 20-seat neighborhood gem has evolved into Manhattan's most sought-after dining destination, while maintaining its commitment to personalized service and exceptional cuisine.

Our philosophy centers on the belief that great dining experiences are created through the harmony of exceptional ingredients, masterful technique, and genuine hospitality. We work exclusively with local farms and artisanal producers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability.
